Our Story

A Sanctuary for the Senses

Velour Café was created as a space where time slows down. In the heart of the city, we've built a sanctuary that celebrates the art of the pause.

Our beans are ethically sourced from small-batch roasters who share our passion for quality and sustainability. Every cup is a testament to our commitment to excellence, served in an atmosphere designed for quiet reflection and creative spark.
